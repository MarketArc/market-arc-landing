Business plan — 5 minute setup

1. Install the extension — Firefox or Edge. Done in 20 seconds.

2. Track your first product — visit any product page, click the extension, hit Track. It's in your dashboard, checked every hour.

3. Track a competitor — on the same product, click "Track as Competitor" and pick which of your products they compete with. Up to 3 per product.

4. Open your Intelligence Matrix — the Competitors tab shows every competitor, their price, the gap, and who's winning. Export to CSV anytime.

5. Wire up webhooks — go to Settings, drop in your endpoint URL. Every time a competitor moves, you get a real-time ping. Zero delay.
